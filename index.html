How to play

Easy to learn. Hard to master.

  • 1
    Tap anywhere on the screen to drop the current block on top of the tower.
  • 2
    Try to align it as close as possible to the block below. Any overhang gets sliced off.
  • 3
    Keep stacking to push your tower higher. Perfect drops give you bonus points and extra satisfying feedback.
  • 4
    Miss too far and the remaining block becomes too small – that’s Game Over. Can you beat your highscore?
Pro Tip

Want a more relaxed or more intense camera? Use the in-game settings to choose if the camera should follow every block or only move up every few stacks.
